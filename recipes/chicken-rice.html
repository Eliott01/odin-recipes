Spicy Chicken and Rice

Description

This easy to make spicy chicken and rice recipe will bring the heat to the table while also making sure to bring the flavour.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 150g of rice
  • Nandos medium marinade
  • 1 tbs herbes de provance
  • 1 tbs paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic granules

Spicy Chicken and Rice

Description

This easy to make spicy chicken and rice recipe will bring the heat to the table while also making sure to bring the flavour.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 150g of rice
  • Nandos medium marinade
  • 1 tbs herbes de provance
  • 1 tbs paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic granules
  • pinch of pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare chicken thighs by cutting off any excess fat and put in a bowl.
  2. Add Nandos marinade and the spices listed to the chicken making sure it is covered.
  3. While the chicken marinates prepare some boiling water for the rice.
  4. Add chicken to a hot pan and cook till golden brown making sure it is cooked through!
  5. Add rice to the boiling water and bring to a simmer cooking for approx 10-12 minutes.
  6. When chicken and rice are both cooked add the rice to the pan and mix together on low heat.
  7. Serve up the food and add any sauce of your choice, Enjoy!

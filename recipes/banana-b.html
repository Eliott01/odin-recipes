Banana Bread

Description

This banana bread recipe creates the most delicious, moist loaf with loads of banana flavor.
Friends and family love my recipe and say it's by far the best! It tastes wonderful warm and cold. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3 over ripped bananas
  • 500g Cake mix
  • 1 large egg
  • 200g butter
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mash the bananas in a bowl until fully broken up.
  2. Add cake mix, butter and egg to the bowl and mix together.
  3. Once mixed together add vanilla extract and mix it in.
  4. Preheat oven at gas mark 4.
  5. Once oven is ready put your mix in a tin and put in the oven for 50 minutes.
  6. Let the banana bread cool down for 15 minutes before serving.
  7. Enjoy!

Breakfast Wrap

Description

This quick and simple recipe is perfect for a delicious breakfast or tasty snack!

Ingredients

  • Large wrap
  • 2-3 bacon rashers
  • 2 large eggs
  • Cheese of your choice

Steps

  1. Heat pans for the eggs and bacon.
  2. Once pans are hot add your bacon and shortly after the eggs.
  3. When both are nearly done add your wrap to the grill and add cheese on top of bacon to melt it.
  4. Take wrap out from the grill and add your bacon, egg and cheese.
  5. Add any sauce you want, wrap it up and enjoy!
